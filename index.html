I am currently an Assisstant Professor at the School of Public Finance and Taxation, Nanjing University of Finance and Economics. I graduated with a PhD in Economics from Center for China Fiscal Development, Central University of Finance and Economics in June 2022.

我的研究领域为：财政与货币关系、宏观政策的微观效应评估、卫生经济与政策。
My research interests include public finance and monetary relation, the evaluation of effects of macro policies on micro entities, and health economics and policy.
